THE FIRM

About Khan & Associates

Khan & Associates is a full-service law firm based in Karachi, advising individuals, family businesses, listed companies and public institutions across Pakistan. The firm was founded on a simple principle: every client deserves counsel that is prepared, candid and relentless.

Since its founding, the firm has appeared before the Supreme Court of Pakistan, the High Courts, banking courts, tribunals and regulatory bodies. Our advocates combine deep courtroom experience with transactional and advisory work, allowing us to see disputes and deals from both sides of the table.

We deliberately keep our client list focused. That means the partner you meet on day one is the advocate who argues your matter, drafts your documents and answers your calls.
