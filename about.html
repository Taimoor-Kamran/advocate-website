THE FIRM

About Us

Founded in 2016 by Advocate Shumaila Sadaf, Billions Works International Law Firm is a modern full-service law firm delivering litigation, corporate advisory, regulatory compliance and legal technology solutions across Pakistan. We represent individuals, businesses, financial institutions and public organizations with professionalism, integrity and strategic legal counsel.

Our firm combines 15+ years of legal experience with expertise in artificial intelligence, digital assets, compliance and emerging technologies, enabling clients to navigate today's evolving legal and regulatory landscape with confidence.

We believe every client deserves practical advice, meticulous preparation and dedicated representation.
