IN DEPTH

Our Practices in Detail

Corporate Registration, Compliance & Litigation

From incorporation to exit, we support companies at every stage of the corporate life cycle. We advise sponsors, family businesses, SMEs and foreign investors on establishing and operating companies in Pakistan under the Companies Act, 2017, and we manage ongoing filings and governance requirements before the Securities and Exchange Commission of Pakistan (SECP). When boardroom or shareholder relationships break down, our advocates represent clients in corporate disputes with the same rigour we bring to compliance work.

  • Company and single-member company incorporation with SECP
  • Drafting of Memorandum and Articles of Association and shareholders' agreements
  • Statutory returns, annual filings and regulatory compliance calendars
  • Corporate governance, board and secretarial advisory
  • Shareholder, director and management disputes
  • Mergers, restructuring and winding-up proceedings

Banking, Finance & AML Compliance

We advise banks, microfinance institutions, exchange companies and fintechs on the regulatory frameworks administered by the State Bank of Pakistan, and we act for both lenders and borrowers in finance transactions and recovery litigation. A core strength of the practice is anti-money-laundering and counter-terrorist-financing compliance: we design and audit AML/CFT programs, train compliance teams and assist institutions in responding to regulator and Financial Monitoring Unit inquiries.

  • AML/CFT compliance programs — KYC/CDD policies, screening and reporting procedures
  • Advisory on State Bank of Pakistan regulations and licensing
  • Finance and security documentation for lenders and borrowers
  • Recovery proceedings under the Financial Institutions (Recovery of Finances) Ordinance, 2001
  • Responses to regulator inspections and Financial Monitoring Unit inquiries
  • Fintech, EMI and digital-banking regulatory support

Taxation & Customs

Our tax team advises businesses and individuals on income tax, sales tax and customs matters — combining planning and advisory work with representation in audits, appeals and references. On the customs side, we handle goods declarations, valuation and classification disputes, and facilitation through the Pakistan Single Window, and we defend clients in customs adjudication and appellate proceedings.

  • Tax planning and advisory for businesses and individuals
  • Responses to FBR notices, audits and assessments
  • Appeals before the Commissioner (Appeals) and Appellate Tribunal Inland Revenue
  • Customs goods declarations (GD), valuation and classification disputes
  • Pakistan Single Window (PSW) and WeBOC facilitation
  • Sales tax registration, compliance and refund claims
Discuss a matter →

Criminal & Customs Litigation

We defend individuals and corporations at every stage of the criminal process — from first information report to bail, trial, appeal and revision. The practice has particular depth in customs prosecutions and regulatory offences, where financial and procedural detail often decides the outcome. Our advocates prepare every defense on the assumption that the matter will be fought to the end.

  • Bail applications before trial and appellate courts
  • Trial defense in criminal proceedings of every kind
  • Appeals and revisions before the High Courts
  • Customs offences, seizures and adjudication proceedings
  • FIA financial crime and cybercrime matters
  • Anti-corruption and accountability proceedings
Discuss a matter →

Arbitration & Mediation

Commercial disputes do not always belong in court. We represent clients in domestic and international arbitrations, both institutional and ad hoc, and we advise on the enforcement and challenge of arbitral awards, including foreign awards under the Recognition and Enforcement (Arbitration Agreements and Foreign Arbitral Awards) Act, 2011. Where relationships can be preserved, we guide parties through mediation and structured settlement negotiation.

  • Drafting and review of arbitration clauses and agreements
  • Representation in domestic and international commercial arbitrations
  • Enforcement and setting aside of arbitral awards
  • Enforcement of foreign awards under the New York Convention framework
  • Mediation, conciliation and structured settlement negotiation
  • Dispute-resolution strategy for ongoing commercial relationships
Discuss a matter →

Virtual Assets Compliance

Pakistan's virtual-asset regulatory landscape is developing quickly, and businesses in this space need advice that keeps pace. We assist exchanges, custodians, token projects and investors with licensing readiness, AML/CFT compliance tailored to digital assets, and engagement with regulators as the framework evolves. Our combination of legal, finance and software-engineering backgrounds is particularly suited to this practice.

  • Licensing and registration readiness for virtual asset service providers
  • AML/CFT compliance programs for digital-asset businesses
  • Token, product and platform structuring reviews
  • Regulatory engagement and no-objection processes
  • Risk assessments and compliance audits
  • Monitoring of regulatory developments and impact briefings

Import & Export Compliance

Cross-border trade in Pakistan runs on registrations, permissions and precise documentation. We help importers and exporters put the right foundations in place — from trade registrations and Chamber of Commerce membership to product-specific approvals — and we resolve the compliance issues that interrupt shipments and payments.

  • Importer and exporter registration through PSW/WeBOC
  • Chamber of Commerce membership and certifications
  • Regulatory permissions, NOCs and product approvals
  • Review of trade documentation and Incoterms allocation of risk
  • Resolution of customs holds, detentions and disputes
  • Advisory on trade policy, quotas and duty structures

Cybersecurity & Digital Forensics

When technology and law collide, clients need advisers fluent in both. We act in cybercrime matters under the Prevention of Electronic Crimes Act, 2016 — for complainants and for the accused — and we support organisations through security incidents with legally sound response, evidence preservation and regulator communication. Our digital-forensics capability helps convert technical findings into admissible evidence.

  • Cybercrime complaints and defense under PECA, 2016
  • Incident response and data-breach advisory
  • Digital forensics and preservation of electronic evidence
  • Data protection and privacy compliance
  • Internal and corporate investigations
  • Expert support for electronic evidence in court

Intellectual Property

A brand or a work is only as strong as its protection. We register and prosecute trademarks and copyrights before IPO-Pakistan, manage oppositions and cancellations, and enforce our clients' rights through infringement actions and anti-counterfeiting measures before the IP Tribunals. We also structure licensing and assignment arrangements so that IP value is captured, not leaked.

  • Trademark searches, filing and prosecution before IPO-Pakistan
  • Copyright registration and protection
  • Opposition, rectification and cancellation proceedings
  • Licensing, franchising and assignment agreements
  • Infringement suits before the IP Tribunals
  • Anti-counterfeiting strategy and enforcement

Property & Real Estate

Property matters in Pakistan demand caution before commitment. We conduct title verification and due diligence ahead of purchases, handle registration and transfer formalities, and advise families on inheritance and succession. Where disputes arise — over possession, partition, tenancy or development — our litigators act before the civil courts and specialised forums.

  • Title verification and pre-purchase due diligence
  • Sale, purchase, transfer and registration of property
  • Inheritance matters and succession certificates
  • Partition and possession suits
  • Tenancy agreements and eviction proceedings
  • Disputes with builders, developers and housing societies

Shariah Compliance

We advise financial institutions and businesses on structuring products and operations in accordance with Shariah principles, drawing on the State Bank of Pakistan's Shariah governance framework and recognised international standards. The practice supports both dedicated Islamic institutions and conventional players developing Islamic windows.

  • Islamic finance documentation — Murabaha, Ijarah, Musharakah and Diminishing Musharakah
  • Shariah governance frameworks and policies
  • Product development and Shariah review support
  • Conversion of conventional products to Shariah-compliant structures
  • Advisory for Islamic banking windows and takaful operators
  • Shariah-compliance audits and remediation

  • Litigation management for overseas Pakistanis
  • Powers of attorney — drafting, attestation and registration
  • Property protection and dispute handling for expatriates
  • Cross-border contracts and international compliance advisory
  • Coordination with foreign counsel and embassies

  • Family Registration Certificates and record corrections
  • Resolution of biometric verification issues
  • Notary Public services and notarisation of documents
  • Attestation of documents for domestic and foreign use
  • Affidavits, declarations and oath administration
